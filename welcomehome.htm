Fellowship Kids is Open!

August 23rd is Promotion Sunday in Fellowship Kids!

On Promotion Sunday, all kids will move up the their new age or grade level group. Starting August 23rd, Fellowship Kids will be available for ages 15-months up to 6th grade.

A few things to note:

  • If you have a fever, cough, shortness of breath, any symptoms of COVID-19 or have recently been exposed to someone with COVID-19, please stay home and join us online.
  • We strongly encourage parents and kids to wear a mask while in the building.
  • Online kids service will be available through the Fellowship Kids YouTube channel. You can subscribe to the channel at YouTube.

Please look closely at the options on the registration form to make sure you select the correct class for your child.
