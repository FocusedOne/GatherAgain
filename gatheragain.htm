Together Again

Welcome Back!

This form allows us to count people planning to attend our worship services. It'll help us know how to set up our rooms and prepare to gather again as a church while under the threat of COVID-19.

A few things to note

  • Fellowship Kids is closed this week. Please read and consider the CDC guidelines on young children in large gatherings.
  • We will seat each family separately as they arrive in observation of the State's social distancing guidelines.
  • We encourage everyone to wear a face mask while in the building.

Church service will certainly look different for the next few weeks. We're delighted to gather together to celebrate the good news of our salvation by grace, through faith in Christ, for the glory of God, together again!
