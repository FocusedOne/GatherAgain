Welcome Back!

Please take a moment and let us know what worship service you and your family will be attending. With limited seating capacity due to COVID-19, you signing up helps us best prepare seating for our worship services.

A few things to note

  • If you have a fever, cough, shortness of breath, any symptoms of COVID-19 or have recently been exposed to someone with COVID-19, please stay home and join us online.
  • Fellowship kids is closed.
  • We encourage you to wear a mask while in the building.
  • We will seat each individual or family as they arrive using proper social distance guidelines.
  • If you are in a high-risk group for COVID-19, please stay home and join us online.

We are excited about you joining us for worship services! We can't wait to watch God make more disciples at home and across the world as we get back together again to worship Him and bring Him glory.
